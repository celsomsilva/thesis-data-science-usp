# Research Paper

This repository is based on the research developed during my MBA in Data Science and Analytics at the University of São Paulo (USP).

The study investigates weekly retail sales using hierarchical statistical modeling, focusing on the application of a nonlinear three-level multilevel model (HLM3) to Walmart sales data.
